# VNN301 — Hanoi & Northern Highlights

Welcome to VNN301, our signature private tour exploring Hanoi and the best of Northern Vietnam. This flexible itinerary can be adjusted to fit your schedule (2–7 days).

## Highlights

- Hanoi Old Quarter walking tour
- Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple
- Ninh Binh or Ha Long Bay day-trip options
- Local street food and culinary experiences

## Sample 3-Day Itinerary

### Day 1 — Hanoi City Discovery
- Morning: Arrival and transfer to hotel
- Afternoon: Walking tour of Hanoi Old Quarter, stop at Hoan Kiem Lake
- Evening: Dinner with a local family (optional)

### Day 2 — Ha Long Bay (Day trip)
- Early Morning: Transfer to Ha Long Bay
- Full Day: Cruise, cave visit, kayaking (optional)
- Evening: Return to Hanoi

### Day 3 — Ninh Binh (Alternative to Ha Long)
- Full Day: Scenic boat ride at Tam Coc / Trang An
- Visit Mua Cave for panoramic views
- Return to Hanoi and depart
