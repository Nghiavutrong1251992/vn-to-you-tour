UNESCO World Heritage ancient town with lanterns, tailors, and timeless charm

Hoi An Ancient Town Overview

Hoi An Ancient Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that perfectly preserves the atmosphere of a Southeast Asian trading port from the 15th to 19th centuries. With over 1,000 historic buildings, the town offers an enchanting blend of Vietnamese, Chinese, Japanese, and European architectural influences.

This comprehensive guide covers the essential Hoi An experiences, from exploring ancient houses and temples to enjoying the famous lantern festival and world-class tailoring services.
