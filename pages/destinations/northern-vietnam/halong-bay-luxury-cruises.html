For those who seek more than just a tour, Halong Bay offers an elite collection of floating hotels. These are not just boats; they are masterpieces of design, featuring Michelin-star-inspired dining, private heated pools, and spa services that rival the best resorts on land.

What Defines a Luxury Cruise in Halong?

In the bay, "Luxury" is measured by several high standards:

  • Space: All cabins must have private balconies and floor-to-ceiling windows.
  • Service: High staff-to-guest ratio (often 1:1) and butler services in premium suites.
  • Cruising Route: Modern luxury ships often cruise through Lan Ha Bay or Bai Tu Long Bay to ensure a more private and serene environment.

Top 10 Premium Cruise Options

Here are our top recommendations for a high-end voyage:

1. Stellar of the Seas

Known for its triangular design and the first mini-golf course on the bay. It features a seasonal swimming pool and ultra-modern suites.

2. Elite of the Seas

The sibling of Stellar, this is a 6-star luxury cruise boasting an infinity pool, a cigar cellar, and some of the largest suites in Vietnam.

3. Heritage Cruises Binh Chuan

For art and history lovers. Inspired by the "King of Ships" Binh Chuan, it is decorated with 1920s Indochine art and antiques.

4. Capella Cruise

Modern architecture meets classic Indochine. It is famous for its "Sea Slide" and infinity pool, making it great for families seeking luxury.

5. Emperor Cruises

An all-inclusive, all-suite cruise with an imperial Vietnamese theme. Every cabin comes with a dedicated butler.

(Other top mentions: Orchid Classic, Indochine Premium, Scarlet Pearl, Ambassador Cruise, and Paradise Elegance).
