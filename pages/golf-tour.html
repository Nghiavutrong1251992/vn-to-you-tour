Vietnam: Asia's Next Premier Golf Destination

Vietnam has rapidly emerged as one of the world's most exciting and desirable golf destinations. The country offers an incredible combination of stunning natural landscapes, world-class course designs by legendary architects, and a rapidly developing tourism infrastructure that caters perfectly to the discerning golf traveler.

From the majestic mountains of the North, the historic coastlines of the Central region, to the vibrant metropolis of the South, Vietnam provides diverse and challenging courses. Whether you seek the classic links experience amidst coastal dunes or a challenging round weaving through tropical parkland, Vietnam promises an unforgettable golf holiday.

Why Vietnam is a Must-Visit for Golfers:

  • World-Class Design: Vietnam boasts courses created by golf legends such as Greg Norman, Sir Nick Faldo, Colin Montgomerie, and Nicklaus Design.
  • Diverse Scenery: Golfers can play against the backdrop of limestone karsts (Hanoi/Ninh Binh), the UNESCO heritage site of Ha Long Bay (Quang Ninh), or the pristine beaches of the Central Coast (Da Nang/Hoi An).
  • Integrated Resorts: Many top courses are integrated within luxury resorts, offering seamless "Stay and Play" experiences with five-star amenities.
  • Warm Hospitality: Vietnamese hospitality is renowned globally, ensuring a welcoming and personalized experience both on and off the course.
